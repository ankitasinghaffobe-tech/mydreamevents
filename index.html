Who we are

A private studio for the world's most considered celebrations

My Dream is a boutique planning house working across continents for couples and companies who want their event to feel inevitable — as if it could not have been designed any other way.

We pair the discipline of hospitality-grade logistics with an editor's eye for detail: hand-selected venues, growers we've known for a decade, and a production team that disappears into the background so you can be present in the room. Every My Dream event is built once, for the people living inside it.
